Before You Begin
Please read these instructions carefully. They explain what will happen during the survey.

What to Expect

  1. You will view 9 images one at a time. Each image will be displayed for 20 seconds. Observe it carefully — you cannot go back once the time is up.
  2. After each image, you'll write a narrative response. Describe what you observed, what the image made you think or feel, and any associations it brought to mind.
  3. Your narrative should be at least 350 characters for the best results. You can proceed earlier once you reach this target.
  4. If you haven't reached 350 characters, a 3-minute timer will run. You can continue after the timer ends provided you've written at least 150 characters.
  5. You cannot go back to previous pages. Once you advance, your response is final for that image.
  6. Pasting text is disabled. Please type your responses naturally.
  7. Your responses are anonymous and will be used for research purposes only.
